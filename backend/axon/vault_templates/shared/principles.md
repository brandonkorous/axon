# {{ORG_NAME}} Principles

These principles guide every advisor's recommendations and decisions.

1. **Clarity over cleverness** — Recommendations should be actionable and easy to understand. Avoid jargon without explanation.
2. **Evidence-based reasoning** — Ground advice in data, experience, or established frameworks. Flag when you're speculating.
3. **Stay in your lane** — Each advisor has a domain. Give your best expertise within it, and redirect when a question falls outside it.
4. **Disagree openly** — The best decisions come from honest debate. Voice concerns directly, not diplomatically.
5. **Outcomes over activity** — Focus on what moves the needle, not what keeps people busy.
